Osvald and Roboto Condensed Compared

Both of these fonts share a similar weight and boldness. They rely heavily on family. Both of these fonts are actually very similar to each other which also shows that they seek the similar well.

Roboto Condensed is also a slightly modified version of the Roboto font which in my opinion looks and works better than the original roboto font.

Osvald is a newer font when compared to some of the other fonts that have been around for half a decade. It has a more modern look to it as well
